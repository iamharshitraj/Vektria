Our Story & Mission

Learn about what drives us and makes Vektria unique

Bridging the Skills Gap

Founded in 2018, Vektria emerged from a simple observation: the rapid evolution of technology was creating a significant gap between academic education and industry requirements. Our mission is to bridge this gap by providing practical, industry-relevant training.

We believe that everyone should have access to quality tech education regardless of their background. Our programs are designed to be accessible, comprehensive, and tailored to meet the demands of today's job market.

At Vektria, we don't just teach skills - we cultivate problem-solvers, innovators, and leaders who will shape the future of technology.

Our Values

We are committed to providing the highest quality education through constantly updated curriculum, expert instructors, and practical learning approaches.

Our Journey

Key milestones in Vektria's history

2018

Foundation

Vektria was founded with a vision to bridge the gap between academic learning and industry requirements.

2019

First Bootcamp

Launched our first web development bootcamp with 50 students, achieving a 92% job placement rate.

2020

Pandemic Pivot

Successfully transitioned to fully online learning, expanding our reach to international students.

2021

AI Program Launch

Introduced our Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning specialization program.

2022

Global Expansion

Expanded our programs to serve students in 12 countries across 4 continents.

2023

5,000 Students

Celebrated reaching the milestone of training over 5,000 students across all programs.
